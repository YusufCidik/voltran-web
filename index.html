WHO WE ARE?

VolTRan Robotics is the official high school robotics team of Pertevniyal High School, based in Istanbul, Turkey. We were established in 2017 as part of the globally renowned FRC (FIRST Robotics Competition), and have been continuing our journey since 2021 in the VEX Robotics Competition. Our team consists of highly motivated students who come together to explore the intersection of science, technology, engineering, and creativity. As of 2023, VolTRan is composed of 20 members, divided into Mechanical, Software, PR, and Management sub-teams. Our mission goes beyond robotics. We aim to cultivate leadership, collaboration, and innovation through hands-on experience and global competition. Every robot we build, every code we write, and every connection we make is driven by a desire to learn and lead.
